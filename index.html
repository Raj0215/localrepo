Welcome To Our E-Library
The Ultimate Search Engine ADCET

The ADCET central Library is one of the biggest and well-equipped libraries in Maharashtra. Library has a strong collection of reading material including Books, Bound Volumes of Periodicals, dissertations and non-print material such as E-Books, E-Journals, CDs, and NPTEL Video Lectures. The Library is fully computerized using Vidyasagar v4.0 LMS and provides Web OPAC and OPAC Mobile App facility to all readers. ‘This library offers total OPEN ACCESS to all readers.’ Central Library has an excellent digital library Section and provides access to E-resources.

About Us

At our library system, we're dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of readers and learners. With a diverse collection of books, digital resources, and engaging programs, we strive to empower individuals of all ages to explore, discover, and grow. Our knowledgeable staff is committed to providing a welcoming environment, promoting literacy, and facilitating lifelong learning. Join us on a journey of intellectual exploration and community connection, where the love of books and knowledge thrives. Welcome to a place where curiosity knows no bounds.
